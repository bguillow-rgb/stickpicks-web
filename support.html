Common Questions

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to the Profile tab, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete Account. Confirm. This permanently deletes your humidor, journal, scan history, ratings, and reviews. You cannot undo this.

If you've lost access to the app but still need to delete your account, email us from the address on your account with the subject line "Delete My Account" and we'll remove your data within 7 days.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Pro is managed by Apple, not by us. On your iPhone or iPad:

  • Open Settings and tap your name at the top.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Tap Stick Picks Pro and then Cancel Subscription.

Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Deleting your account in the app does not automatically cancel an active subscription — cancel separately.

The scanner can't identify my cigar. What can I try?

  • Hold the band so the brand name and line text are clearly legible.
  • Shoot in good light; avoid glare and motion blur.
  • If one side of the band is obscured, rotate and re-shoot. Multiple frames help.
  • Tap Enhance and retry after a failure — we'll re-analyze with an OCR-biased prompt.
  • Still stuck? Use Find It Manually or Suggest a Cigar and we'll add it to the catalog.

How old do I have to be to use the app?

Stick Picks is intended for adults aged 21 and older, matching US federal legal smoking age. A 21+ age gate is enforced on first launch.

Is Stick Picks a retailer?

No. Stick Picks is a reference and journal app for cigar collectors. We do not sell cigars, distribute tobacco products, or promote tobacco consumption. Our focus is on cataloging, organizing, and tracking the value of a collection.
